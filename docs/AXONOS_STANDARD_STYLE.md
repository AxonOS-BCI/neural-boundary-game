# AxonOS Standard Foundation Grande Style

The visual system must be:

- calm;
- dark;
- precise;
- premium;
- readable;
- grid-aligned;
- free of skewed text;
- free of decorative noise;
- understandable in ten seconds.

Rules:

- no rotated labels;
- no crowded microcopy;
- no random glow;
- no cheap neon;
- no fake complexity;
- every panel needs a reason;
- every line must support hierarchy.
